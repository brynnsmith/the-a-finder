Abortion is Healthcare

"Like all medical matters, decisions regarding abortion should be made by patients in consultation with their healthcare providers, and without undue interference by outside parties. Those obtaining abortion should be entitled to privacy, dignity, respect and support."

With the recent SCOTUS decision to overturn Roe v Wade, some states have banned or restricted access to abortions. We strongly believe that everyone should have safe and unrestricted access to abortion care. Here are some tips on finding the closest clinic locations, requesting funding, protecting your privacy online, accessing at-home abortion pills and information, finding mental health and legal support, as well as a list of some orgaizations to donate to.
